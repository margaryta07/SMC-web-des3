Meet Our Head Designer
Born in England and raised Stateside, Julia Bar followed her curiosities from San Francisco to NYC and back. After being immersed in costume design and theatre school, she earned a Master of Arts in fashion history from New York University and holds an Applied Jewelry Professional diploma from the Gemological Institute of America (GIA).
“I wanted to create a line of wearable, everyday fine jewelry that flirts with the past and feels just right for the present. And I put care into choosing materials that are kinder to people and the planet: Recycled gold, ethically sourced gems, and eco-friendly packaging. My hope is that these are pieces you’ll feel good in – and materials you’ll feel good about – for years to come.” – Julia Bar, Head Designer
