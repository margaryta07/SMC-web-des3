Meet Our Team

Kyle Blake

President & Owner

Kyle Blake has more than 25 years of experience in the landscaping industry. Kyle has been the leader of GreenSweep since the company's modest beginnings when he and his brother were mowing lawns in their neighborhood. His knowledge of the industry has continued to develop through his years at the helm of the GreenSweep where he's been able to make something bigger out of his childhood passion for landscaping. Kyle's understanding of landscape maintenance and installation is only surpassed by his desire to exceed client expectations.

Kyle holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Landscape Contracting with a concentration in Business from Pennsylvania State University. Kyle is a MD Licensed Tree Expert, Certified Fertilizer Applicator, and Certified Pesticide Applicator. Kyle also owns Garden Gate Landscaping.

The Team

Meet the people who make GreenSweep Work

Elizabeth King

Marketing Director

Elizabeth is responsible for external and internal communications while promoting the company’s services to existing and prospective customers, employees, and the community. She manages content creation for our social media platforms and promotional materials. She is also responsible for community outreach, special projects and event planning at GreenSweep.

Alex Bell

Director of Landscape Construction

Alex oversees all landscape construction operations, which includes scheduling, personnel, logistics and customer satisfaction on all our hardscape and landscape installation projects. Alex joined GreenSweep in early 2021 as an Account Manager. Prior to joining GreenSweep, Alex spent several years with a landscaping company in Rhode Island. Alex has strong customer service skills and field experience which will aid him in his role.

Jedd Turner

Vice President of Design & Development

Jedd rejoined the GreenSweep team in 2018 to lead efforts in landscape design and business development. Jedd helps navigate customers through the landscape design process – gathering ideas, reviewing budget, and finalizing a cohesive design – to meet each client’s goals for their outdoor space.
